1.  SCOPE OF SUPPLY
================================================================================

1.1  Seller agrees to sell and Buyer agrees to purchase the goods described
     below (the "Goods"):

     Item:         Xilinx Kintex-7 FPGA Development Boards (Model: KC705)
     Quantity:     45 units
     Unit Price:   $3,495.00 USD
     Total Value:  $157,275.00 USD

1.2  The Goods shall be used by Buyer for research and development purposes
     at Buyer's Signal Processing Laboratory. Specific end-use: development
     of high-speed digital signal processing algorithms for radar signal
     analysis applications.

1.3  Seller shall provide technical documentation, design files, and
     reference implementations necessary for Buyer's integration of the
     FPGA boards into its signal processing systems.

================================================================================
2.  DELIVERY TERMS
================================================================================

2.2  Buyer shall arrange and pay for all freight, insurance, and customs
     clearance from Seller's facility to Buyer's designated receiving
     location in Tehran, Iran.

2.3  Estimated ship date: September 15, 2024, or within 30 days of receipt
     of payment, whichever is later.

================================================================================
3.  PAYMENT TERMS
================================================================================

4.3  All technical data, documentation, and software provided under this
