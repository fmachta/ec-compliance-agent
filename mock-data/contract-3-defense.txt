================================================================================
              RE-EXPORT AUTHORIZATION AND TRANSFER AGREEMENT
================================================================================

Agreement No:   RTA-2024-0309
Date:           March 9, 2024
Parties:        Transferor:  OptiDefense Systems, LLC (San Diego, CA, USA)
                Transferee:  Al-Jazari Defense Industries (Riyadh, Saudi Arabia)

================================================================================
1.  RECITALS
================================================================================

1.1  WHEREAS, Transferee has previously acquired from Transferor certain
     night-vision and thermal imaging components ("Original Export"),
     originally exported under US State Department authorization;

1.2  WHEREAS, Transferee now desires to transfer a portion of such
     components to its subsidiary, Al-Jazari Defense Industries — Gulf
     Division (Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates), for integration into
     armored vehicle targeting systems;

1.3  WHEREAS, Transferor is willing to authorize such transfer subject to
     the terms and conditions set forth herein.

================================================================================
2.  ITEMS SUBJECT TO TRANSFER
================================================================================

2.1  The items authorized for re-transfer ("Items") are:

     (a) Model XR-7 Gen III+ Image Intensifier Tubes (Qty: 120 units)
         USML Category XII(b)
     (b) Model TIS-9500 Thermal Imaging Sensor Cores (Qty: 85 units)
         USML Category XII(c)
     (c) Integrated Night-Vision Monocular Assemblies (Qty: 75 units)
         USML Category XII(d)
     (d) Associated technical documentation and maintenance manuals

2.2  Total notional value of Items: $3,450,000 USD

================================================================================
3.  TRANSFER AUTHORIZATION
================================================================================

3.1  Transferor hereby authorizes Transferee to transfer the Items from
     Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, to Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates.

3.2  The Items shall be integrated into Al-Jazari's ASV-90 Armored
     Surveillance Vehicle platform for use by the United Arab Emirates
     Armed Forces.

================================================================================
4.  CONDITIONS OF TRANSFER
================================================================================

4.1  Transferee shall ensure that the Items are used exclusively for the
     purposes described in Section 3.2.

4.2  Transferee shall not further transfer, re-export, or re-sell the Items
     to any other party without Transferor's prior written consent.

4.3  Transferee shall maintain accurate records of the Items' location,
     custody, and use for a period of three (3) years.

================================================================================
5.  FEES
================================================================================

5.1  In consideration of Transferor's authorization, Transferee shall pay
     a Transfer Authorization Fee of $175,000 USD within 15 days of
     execution of this Agreement.

================================================================================
6.  COMPLIANCE
================================================================================

6.1  Transferee acknowledges that the Items are controlled under the
     International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR) and agrees to comply
     with all applicable requirements.

6.2  Transferee shall indemnify and hold harmless Transferor from any
     claims, penalties, or liabilities arising from Transferee's failure
     to comply with ITAR or other applicable export control laws.

================================================================================
7.  GOVERNING LAW
================================================================================

7.1  This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of
     California, USA.
