2.  SCOPE OF SERVICES
================================================================================

2.1  Contractor shall provide the following services:

     (a) Computational fluid dynamics (CFD) modeling and simulation of

     (b) Analysis of composite material behavior under extreme thermal
         and mechanical stress conditions
     (c) Development of optimization algorithms for turbine cooling
         channel geometries
     (d) Documentation of simulation methodologies and results

2.2  Contractor shall have full access to Company's proprietary CFD
     software suite, engineering databases, and technical documentation
     necessary to perform the Services.

2.3  Contractor shall have access to Company's internal network, including
     servers hosting controlled technical data related to turbine engine
     components regulated under ECCN 9E003.
